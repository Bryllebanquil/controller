Watchdog monitors every 10 seconds:

🔍 HOW TO VERIFY

Check AppData (Hidden):
  dir /a %LOCALAPPDATA%\Microsoft\Windows\svchost.exe

Check Startup (Visible):
  Win+R → shell:startup
  Look for: WindowsSecurityUpdate.exe

  1. Delete either file
  2. Wait 10 seconds
  3. Check again → File restored!
